About Gyan Srajan Infrastructure Projects Pvt. Ltd.

Gyan Srajan Infrastructure Projects Pvt. Ltd. is a multidisciplinary engineering and construction company committed to delivering reliable, sustainable and high-quality infrastructure solutions across India. Established in 2023, the company combines experienced leadership with modern engineering practices to deliver projects that meet the highest standards of quality, safety and efficiency.

Our expertise includes structural engineering, infrastructure development, structural retrofitting and rehabilitation, rainwater harvesting systems, project management consultancy and turnkey construction projects. We believe every project deserves technical excellence, professional commitment and attention to detail.
